Shri Dhirajlal Makawana

President

When I established Navjeevan High School in 1980, my dream was simple yet powerful—to provide quality education to every child, regardless of their background, and to uplift our rural community through knowledge, values, and discipline.

In those early days, we began with just a few classes and limited resources, but our hearts were filled with dedication and hope. Step by step, with the support of like-minded individuals, dedicated teachers, and the trust of our people, DSC grew into the respected institution it is today—offering education from Nursery to Grade 12 in both English and Gujarati mediums.

My focus has always been on creating a school that goes beyond textbooks—a place where character is built, dreams are nurtured, and values are lived every day.

Though times have changed, our mission remains the same: to light the lamp of education in every home, and to ensure that no child is left behind.
