Mrs. Aesha Makawana

Principal

As the Principal of DSC Public School, I feel truly honored to be part of an institution that stands for excellence in education and the holistic development of every child. With a passion for teaching and learning, I have dedicated my journey to creating a school environment where students not only achieve academic success but also grow as confident, kind, and responsible individuals.

I strongly believe that every child is unique, and education should bring out the best in them. At DSC, we focus on value-based education, along with modern teaching techniques, to help students face today’s challenges while preparing them for tomorrow’s opportunities. With the support of our dedicated staff, I work every day to ensure that our classrooms are dynamic, inclusive, and inspiring.

Over the years, I’ve taken great pride in helping the school grow from strength to strength—academically, culturally, and socially. From early years to senior secondary, our focus remains on building a strong foundation, developing critical thinking, and instilling values like respect, responsibility, and resilience.

As an educator and as a mother, I understand the importance of trust and communication between the school and parents. I strive to maintain an open and warm relationship with every family that becomes a part of the DSC community. I am committed to continuing our school’s legacy and shaping it into a model institution not just in Gir Somnath, but across the state.
